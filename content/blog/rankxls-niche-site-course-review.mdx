### What Is RankXL's Niche Site Course

It's a well-documented, step-by-step system that the creator Chris Lee used to build and grow a profitable niche site to over $15,000 a month with Adsense from the ground up.


His approach to building sites is the total opposite of what me and most bloggers in the community are teaching.

Instead of trying to rank a small site for 2-3 small low competitive keywords, Chris started his site by focusing only on the biggest keywords (50,000+ searches a month) and putting all the effort in ranking those.

**Here's the breakdown of his process:**

1.  Target the biggest keywords in the niche with Pillar Articles.
2.  Consistently build links to these pages targeting the big keywords.
3.  Build out the site with inner articles.
4.  Pull in tons of long-tail traffic.
5.  Not right away, but eventually rankings will rise for those big keywords.​
