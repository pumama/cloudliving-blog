## Business Ventures

Currently, all of the business ventures I’m doing are under [TK Ventures](https://tk.ventures), a company that I co-founded with 3 good friends since 2020.

We started mainly as a media company then quickly grew into a portfolio of different products & services aimed at web3 users. Some notable projects:

### Crypto Media Network

When I joined crypto back in 2020, I didn’t really want to only focus on trading.

My goal was to build a sustainable business in crypto.

After some research, I saw an opportunity in media and content creation.

General crypto news was everywhere. But niche media for each crypto ecosystem?

Almost nonexistent.

So, I launched [BSC Daily](https://twitter.com/bsc_daily), the first Twitter channel covering news and updates exclusively for Binance Smart Chain.

To my amazement, BSC Daily skyrocketed, reaching 100,000 followers in just a few months. Our revenue started flowing in through advertising and affiliate channels. It was a confirmation that our business model was on the right track.

So we quickly expanded and started more channels covering most of the major blockchain ecosystems like Ethereum, Solana, Avalanche, and more.

At the moment, our network of channels have more than 2,000,000 followers, reaching tens of millions of impressions.

We also have a few other media projects, such as:

-   [Coinwire](https://coinwire.com) – a crypto news website. This is a very competitive space, so the site is growing slowy & but gradually.
-   [Chainplay.gg](https://chainplay.gg) – a web3 gaming portal. Similar to Coingecko, but targeted only at web3 gaming.
-   [5 Minutes Crypto](https://www.youtube.com/@5PhutCrypto) – one of the biggest crypto & finance Youtube channels in Vietnam.

### Web3 VC Fund

In May 2021, TK Ventures started a small fund using our own money to invest in seed & private sales of 60+ crypto projects. This was a huge success and we were able to return a few hundred percents for our investments.

However, the fund hasn’t deployed money into any new deal since 2022 because I haven’t found many good deals for our criteria yet.

I’ll write a post about my current thesis for investing in crypto soon.
